# Meal Planning Guide

## The Sunday Prep Method
1. Pick 3-4 proteins for the week
2. Pick 2-3 grains/starches
3. Pick 4-5 vegetables
4. Cook proteins and grains in bulk Sunday afternoon
5. Assemble meals daily — mix and match components

## Budget Rules
- Buy whole chickens (cheaper per pound, makes stock from bones)
- Beans and lentils are the cheapest protein on earth
- Frozen vegetables are just as nutritious as fresh
- Seasonal produce is always cheaper
- Plan around what's on sale, not the other way around

## Batch Cooking Winners
- Chili (freezes perfectly, better next day)
- Soups and stews (same — freeze in portions)
- Rice (cook a big batch, refrigerate, use all week)
- Roasted vegetables (cook once, eat in salads/bowls/wraps)
- Hard-boiled eggs (prep a dozen, snacks all week)

## Avoiding Food Waste
- FIFO: First In, First Out (use oldest ingredients first)
- Wilting herbs → make pesto or chimichurri
- Overripe bananas → freeze for smoothies or banana bread
- Stale bread → croutons, breadcrumbs, French toast
- Vegetable scraps → stock bag in freezer
