# Nutrition Fundamentals

## Macronutrients
- **Protein** (4 cal/g): Muscle repair, satiety. Sources: meat, fish, eggs, legumes, dairy. Aim: 0.7-1g per pound of body weight for active people.
- **Carbohydrates** (4 cal/g): Primary energy source. Complex carbs (whole grains, vegetables) over simple (sugar, white bread). Fiber is a carb — aim for 25-30g/day.
- **Fat** (9 cal/g): Hormone production, vitamin absorption. Prioritize unsaturated (olive oil, avocado, nuts) over saturated. Never zero-fat diets.

## Micronutrients
- **Iron**: Red meat, spinach, lentils. Vitamin C increases absorption.
- **Calcium**: Dairy, leafy greens, fortified foods. Need vitamin D for absorption.
- **B12**: Animal products only — vegans must supplement.
- **Omega-3**: Fatty fish (salmon, sardines), walnuts, flaxseed.

## Practical Rules
1. Half your plate should be vegetables
2. Protein at every meal
3. Drink water before you think you're hungry
4. Whole foods over processed — always
5. No single food is magic or poison — it's the overall pattern that matters
