# Mini Sessions

## What They Are
Short (15-20 minute), affordable portrait sessions — typically themed or seasonal. You book multiple back-to-back on one day at one location. High volume, lower per-session price, but strong total revenue.

## Why They Matter
- **Client acquisition:** Families try you at a low price point, then book full sessions.
- **Cash flow:** A single mini day can gross $2,000-5,000+.
- **Marketing engine:** Dozens of families sharing your work on social = organic reach.
- **Fills your calendar** during slower months or between wedding season.

## Common Mini Session Themes
- **Fall minis:** October/November. Colored leaves, pumpkins, hay bales, warm tones.
- **Holiday/Christmas minis:** November/December. Trees, lights, wreaths, plaid blankets, letter to Santa.
- **Valentine's minis:** February. Red/pink props, hearts, "love" theme for couples and kids.
- **Spring/Easter minis:** March/April. Bunnies (real or stuffed), pastels, flowers, baskets.
- **Back to school:** August/September. Chalkboard, books, backpack, first-day signs.
- **Mommy & me:** Mother's Day. Flowy dresses, studio or garden.
- **Pet minis:** Any season. Bandanas, treats, parks. Always popular.

## How to Structure a Mini Day

### Logistics
- **Session length:** 15-20 minutes. No more. Pad 10 min between for transitions.
- **Sessions per day:** 8-15 depending on stamina. 10 is the sweet spot.
- **One location:** Don't move. Set up your backdrop/area once.
- **One setup:** Same general posing zone. Vary angles and crops for variety.
- **Deliver 15-25 edited images per session.**

### Pricing
- $150-300 per mini (market dependent).
- Include digital gallery only. Upsell prints and albums after.
- Require full payment at booking. No-shows are revenue killers.
- Offer an add-on: +$50 for 5 extra minutes or additional outfit change.

## Marketing Mini Sessions
- Announce 4-6 weeks in advance.
- Instagram teaser posts + stories with behind-the-scenes from last year.
- Email your past client list FIRST (early access / loyalty discount).
- Facebook event or booking page with real-time slot availability.
- Partner with a local boutique or venue for cross-promotion.

## Common Mistakes
- **Too long:** 15-20 min is the limit. You'll burn out and fall behind schedule.
- **Too cheap:** Don't undercut yourself. Minis should be profitable per hour, not charity.
- **No contract:** Yes, even for minis. Online booking with terms = your contract.
- **Over-editing:** Batch edit with a preset. Fine-tune the hero shots. Don't hand-edit 250 images from a mini day.
- **Not upselling:** After delivery, offer prints, canvases, holiday cards. That's where the real margin is.

## Editing Workflow for Minis
- Cull aggressively. 15-25 final images per session.
- Apply a consistent preset across the whole day (same light, same location).
- Fine-tune exposure and white balance per session (light shifts through the day).
- Turnaround: 1-2 weeks. Faster = happier clients who share sooner.
